Baby Panda

For the first 6 to 8 weeks of life, baby panda bears cannot open their eyes, and they are virtually immobile until they reach 3 months of age.

  • Scientific Name : Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length : 3 to 5 ounces
  • Average Lifespan : 14 to 20 years
  • Habitat : Mountainous regions of central China

Baby pandas are born pink with no hair. Pink, hairless, and blind, the cub is 1/900th the size of its mother. As they grow, gradually they turn black and white.
